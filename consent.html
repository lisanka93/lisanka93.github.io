CONSENT FORM

By pressing consent in the MS Form, the participants are consenting to the following points:

  • 1. having read and understood the description of the study, and having had my questions answered satisfactorily
  • 2. understanding that the data gathered will be stored anonymously and securely. It will not be possible to identify them in any publications
  • 3. understanding that their provided answers may be subject to review by responsible individuals from UCL
  • 4. understanding that participation is voluntary and that they are free to withdraw at any time without giving a reason and understanding that if they decide to withdraw any personal data they have provided will be deleted.
  • 5. understanding that after the 1 st of September 2020 no withdrawal requests will be accepted anymore because the dataset with the arguments will be made publicly available for other researchers.
  • 6. understanding that data will not be made available to any commercial organisations but is solely the responsibility of the researcher
  • 7. understanding that they will be compensated for the portion of time spent in the study
  • 8. agreeing that their anonymised research data may be used by others for future research
  • 9. knowing whom to contact if they wish to lodge a complaint
